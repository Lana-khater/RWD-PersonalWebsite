Zen4

Laundry Application

The app includes customer profiles with personal data such as a customer’s name, and phone number. The customer app enables users to:

  • -Sign up and log in (signed up and approved by the admin and recorded uses for future needs)
  • -Choose the type of service and add details (Clothes colours, kinds of clothes, detergent preferences, etc.)
  • -Calculate the cost of service and pay on site (Drying and Washing prices vary by weight and options mentioned above^)
  • -Track time and get notifications (An Invoice is printed due to the user need and a notifaction is sent when the Clothes are done)
